More about the food

There is a famous coastal city in north China, where foreigners worldwide make the town vibrant. There are hundreds of western-style restaurants. I went to some famous European restaurants and the food are very beautiful. The food is also excellent, and I recorded them with my camera.


On this website, food is more than just food, it's about memories. I shared the food with my friends and we talked about people, family, and friends. Since then, the pandemic has changed how we live. For a long time, I have not been able to visit them in person. Seeing these pictures reminded me of those good times.


We have the chance to sit in the restaurants next to the beach and listen to music from different countries. The food is beautiful, and the wind is comfortable. People come here to meet their family and friends, read a book or swim in the sea. After one year, the restaurants were shut down because of the pandemic, which is a different scene.


Joyce Xu-2022
